# Rules

Principles and Patterns for Quality Software

---

## What Are Rules?

Rules are the **principles and patterns** that guide our software development decisions.

They help us:

- Make consistent, informed choices
- Avoid common pitfalls
- Build maintainable systems
- Communicate effectively with other developers

---

## Topics Covered

- **Design Principles** - SOLID, DRY, KISS, YAGNI
- **Architectural Patterns** - Layered, microservices, event-driven
- **Code Organization** - Modularity, coupling, cohesion
- **Best Practices** - Naming conventions, error handling, documentation
