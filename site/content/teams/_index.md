# Teams

Collaboration and Communication

---

## Why Teams Matter

Software is built by **people working together**.

Effective teams:

- Communicate clearly and frequently
- Share knowledge and expertise
- Collaborate to solve problems
- Support each other's growth

---

## Topics Covered

- **Collaboration** - Code reviews, pair programming, knowledge sharing
- **Communication** - Documentation, meetings, feedback
- **Team Dynamics** - Roles, responsibilities, agile practices
- **Culture** - Building trust, psychological safety, continuous improvement
- **Leadership** - Mentoring, decision-making, conflict resolution
