# Philosophy

Software Engineering as Problem Solving

---

## The Approach

Software engineering is fundamentally about **solving problems using software**.

To build high-quality solutions, we need three essential components:

- **Rules** - Principles and patterns that guide our decisions
- **Tools** - Technologies and practices that enable our work
- **Teams** - People who collaborate to deliver solutions

---

## Problem-Solving Mindset

Understanding the problem is the first step to creating quality software.

This section explores:

- How to approach software development as problem-solving
- Core philosophies that lead to successful outcomes
- The mindset needed to build high-quality software
